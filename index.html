About me

I am a front-end web developer, passionate about user-friendly design. For me web development is like creating a painting, just instead of paint, I would use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I seek elegant solutions for both my designs and my code.

As a Google Developer Nanodegree Scholarship recipient at Udacity, I acquired knowledge on many contemporary Web Development subjects, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6 syntax, object-oriented JavaScript, ReactJS, Progressive Web Applications, Accessibility and much more.

I have a strong International Business background and believe that my previous experience and new skills result in effective combination. I have more than 4 years of working experience in international companies with a focus on customer service and effective business collaboration. I am enthusiastic about technologies and willing to share my time, knowledge, and a good mood with the others.
