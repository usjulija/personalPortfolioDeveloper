About me

Hi! My name is Julia and I'm the front-end-web-developer. I have strong background in International Business and Web-Development is the next step in my career. I'm in love with visual arts and technologies. I specialize in responsive & mobile-first design. My aim is to integrate creative elegance into every project, along with intuitive user interface.

I have achieved Udacity This Front End Developer Nanodegree and I have skills to build responsive, dynamic user interfaces on the web. I have strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript therefore, I can manage all client-side scripting.
